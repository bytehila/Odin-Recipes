Jollof Rice

Description

A feast for both the eyes and the taste buds this beautifully prepared Jollof rice takes center stage, rich in color and flavor. The golden-orange grains are perfectly infused with a blend of tomatoes, peppers, and spices, creating that signature smoky, savory taste Nigerians know and love. Paired with tender, spicy chicken and garnished with fresh herbs, the dish is surrounded by an inviting spread of sides, all set against the warm glow of candlelight.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Yellow bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Scotch Bonnet
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chicken
  • Cooking Oil
  • Seasoning Cubes
  • Salt
  • Fresh Herbs(for garnish)

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, onions, and scotch bonnet into a smooth mix.
  2. Fry the blend in oil with tomato paste until it thickens.
  3. Season with cubes, salt, and spices.
  4. Add washed rice, stir, and cook with broth until soft and fluffy.
  5. Fry or grill chicken, season well.
  6. Serve rice hot, garnish with herbs, and enjoy with chicken.
