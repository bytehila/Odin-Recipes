Spaghetti and sauced shrimps

Description

A vibrant plate of spaghetti tossed in a rich tomato-based sauce and paired with juicy prawns, this dish is both simple and elegant. The golden strands of pasta are perfectly coated with sauce, while the prawns add a touch of seafood sweetness and depth. Finished with fresh herbs, it is a meal that balances flavor, texture, and presentation beautifully.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Shrimps/Prawns
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fresh Herbs (basil/parsley)
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente, then drain.
  2. In olive oil, saut√© garlic lightly.
  3. Add prawns and cook until pink.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss in the spaghetti and mix until well coated.
  6. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot.
